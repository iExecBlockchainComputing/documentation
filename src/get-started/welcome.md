## 🤔 Why iExec?

You shouldn't have to choose between innovation and privacy. **Every developer
deserves the power** to build groundbreaking applications while **keeping user
data completely secure**. Your users deserve **absolute confidence** that their
privacy is protected.

  <p class="!m-0">iExec solves this with <span class="text-fuchsia-700 font-semibold">Turnkey Privacy</span> - a toolkit that simplifies data protection, management, and secure computation, even in untrusted environments. iExec believes privacy should be <span class="text-fuchsia-700 font-semibold">plug-and-play simple</span>, not an afterthought.</p>
